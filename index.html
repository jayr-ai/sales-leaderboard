Calls booked, held and no show are counted by the date each call's outcome was last updated on the pipeline board, not the date it was originally scheduled, GHL does not expose a full stage history. Show = calls held plus everything that progressed further (high priority, closed won). Cash is always the real Stripe figure, never GHL's flat close-won stamp. Outbound, inbound and call hours count only this closer's own dialed calls, setter and admin dialing is tracked separately and excluded from these three columns. Trend compares this window to the immediately prior equivalent window (this week vs last week, and so on); All Time never has a prior period.
